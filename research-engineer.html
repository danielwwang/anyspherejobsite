Research Engineer

We're looking for a Research Engineer to push the boundaries of AI-assisted coding. You'll work on the cutting edge of language models, code understanding, and human-AI interaction.

What you'll do:

  • Design and implement novel AI models for code understanding and generation
  • Research new approaches to human-AI collaboration in software development
  • Build prototypes and run experiments to validate research hypotheses
  • Collaborate with product engineers to integrate research into Cursor
  • Publish research findings and represent Cursor in the research community

What we're looking for:

  • PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field (or equivalent experience)
  • Experience with transformer models and large language models
  • Strong programming skills in Python and PyTorch/TensorFlow
  • Track record of research publications in top-tier venues
  • Passion for improving developer productivity through AI
